Ways to Spread Awareness

  • Schools: Integrate age-appropriate nutrition into classes and meals.
  • Families: Provide practical tips for balanced, budget-friendly meals.
  • Communities: Host workshops and cooking demos with local leaders.
  • Social Media: Share bite-sized, culturally relevant guidance.

Small, consistent improvements—like including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins—build lifelong healthy habits.
