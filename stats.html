Many Filipinos face a paradox where they are overweight yet nutritionally deficient‚Äîso-called "hidden hunger." Diets heavy in processed and calorie-dense food often lack essential vitamins and minerals, particularly among low-income populations. 2024 studies show this affects up to 65% of overweight individuals.
